Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026
✅ Good news — your privacy is fully protected

Herbora does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your preferences and favorites are stored locally on your device only.

1. 📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Herbora ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the Herbora application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Herbora is built with a privacy-first approach — the App functions entirely offline and without any user account system.

2. 📦 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Health or medical data you view in the App
  • Contacts, camera, microphone, or any sensor data
🚫 No accounts 🚫 No tracking 🚫 No analytics 🚫 No ads
3. 💾 Local Storage

Herbora stores certain data locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage. This includes:

  • Your selected language preference (Romanian / English)
  • Your saved favorite herbs and remedies
  • Dark mode preference

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

4. 🌐 Third-Party Services

Herbora loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This is a standard CDN request and may result in Google receiving your IP address as part of the standard HTTP request.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The App does not make any other external network requests.

5. 👶 Children's Privacy

Herbora does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no user accounts or data submission mechanisms, so no data from any user — of any age — is collected.

6. 🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the App and on the GitHub repository. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly.
