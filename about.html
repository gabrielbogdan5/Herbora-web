🌱 What is Herbora?

Herbora is a beautifully crafted mobile-first web application dedicated to the world of medicinal herbs and natural remedies. It brings together centuries of plant-based healing wisdom in a clean, accessible format.

Whether you're a herbalism enthusiast, a curious learner, or someone looking for natural wellness guidance, Herbora is your pocket companion for the plant kingdom.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe that knowledge about medicinal plants should be freely accessible to everyone. Herbora is built as an open-source project with no paywalls, no ads, and no tracking — just pure, honest information.

Our goal is to help people reconnect with nature's pharmacy while always encouraging responsible use and professional medical guidance.
